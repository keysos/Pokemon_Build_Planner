How to Use

Use the filters at the top to search for Pokémon by type, then pick one for each slot in your team grid.

Each card lets you set that Pokémon's moves, item, and ability using the dropdowns. Move and item boxes are color-coded by type so it's easy to scan your team at a glance.

Team Defence

This panel shows how well your team holds up against incoming attacks of each type. Hover over a type badge to see which of your Pokémon are weak or resistant to it. Each type is scored as follows:

Type Effectiveness Against You Points
No effect +1.5
0.25x effective +1.5
0.5x effective +1
1x effective 0
2x super effective -1
4x super effective -1.5

Team Type Coverage

This panel shows how well your team can hit each type offensively. Hover over a type badge to see which of your Pokémon and moves are super effective (2x or 4x) against it.

Note: status moves don't deal damage, so they aren't counted toward this coverage.

Use the toggle switch to flip between light and dark mode — your choice is just for this session.

When you're happy with your team, use Export to save it, and Import to load a previously saved team back in.
